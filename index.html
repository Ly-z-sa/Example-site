Delicious Recipes

Breakfast

Pancakes

Fluffy homemade pancakes with syrup.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
Instructions:
  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add wet ingredients and stir until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and pour batter to form pancakes.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  5. Serve with syrup and toppings.

Omelette

Classic omelette with cheese and vegetables.

Ingredients:
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced vegetables
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Whisk eggs and milk in a bowl.
  2. Heat a pan and pour the mixture.
  3. Add cheese and vegetables on top.
  4. Cook until set, then fold and serve.

Lunch

Grilled Chicken

Juicy grilled chicken with herbs.

Ingredients:
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Marinate chicken with spices and oil.
  2. Preheat grill and cook for 6-7 minutes per side.
  3. Let rest before serving.
