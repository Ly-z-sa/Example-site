Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes with syrup.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Heat a pan and pour batter in small circles.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.
  5. Serve with syrup and toppings of choice.

Omelette

A delicious omelette with vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese

Instructions:

  1. Beat eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a pan and pour in the egg mixture.
  3. Add vegetables and cheese on top.
  4. Cook until edges are set, then fold in half.
  5. Serve hot.
